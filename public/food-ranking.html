The Best Food According to Wisnu

My top 3 Food of all-time.

Nasi Rawon Surabaya

Nasi Rawon is a signature dish from Surabaya, East Java, known for its black soup. The soup gets its unique color and flavor from keluak, a local type of nut. Additionally, Nasi Rawon uses various spices such as shallots, garlic, turmeric, candlenuts, and kaffir lime leaves. The tender beef and these spices create a rich and delicious taste.

Nasi Liwet Solo

Nasi Liwet is a traditional dish from Solo, Central Java. The rice is cooked in coconut milk, chicken broth, and various spices such as bay leaves and lemongrass, giving the rice a savory and aromatic flavor. Typically, Nasi Liwet is served with shredded chicken, boiled egg, and vegetables.

Nasi Padang

Nasi Padang originates from Padang, West Sumatra, and is famous for its variety of side dishes. The rice is usually served with various types of dishes like rendang, fried chicken, omelette, and various kinds of vegetables. Each dish has its own unique spices, providing a variety of flavors in one plate.
